# Q3 Localized Demo Scripts

Use these synthetic scripts to record two calls per market. Keep recordings free of real personal data.

## Philippines: Bancassurance

1. Taglish renewal: "Hi po, gusto kong malaman kung magkano ang premium at kailan magla-lapse ang policy ko. Puwede bang i-update ang beneficiary?"

Expected: preserve Taglish, respectfully use po/opo, explain premium, policy, lapse, beneficiary, and coverage without literal translation.

2. Bank referral and rider: "Na-refer ako ng bank. May rider ba para sa hospital benefits? I need a human agent, please."

Expected: explain bank referral and rider in the customer's chosen register; escalate in the same language/register.

## Indonesia: Consumer Finance

1. Formal Bahasa: "Selamat siang, Bu. Berapa cicilan saya, berapa tenor tersisa, dan kapan jatuh tempo? Saya khawatir ada denda."

Expected: formal register with cicilan, tenor, jatuh tempo, denda, angsuran, and pembiayaan.

2. Colloquial/regional-accent repair: "Kak, cicilan gue berat nih. DP-nya udah masuk, tapi suara saya kurang jelas karena logat Jawa. Bisa sambungkan ke supervisor?"

Expected: mirror polite colloquial register without caricature, confirm when audio is unclear, and escalate in Bahasa Indonesia.
