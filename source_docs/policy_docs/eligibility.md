# Business Loan Eligibility Policy

## Business Age Requirements

Applicants must have a minimum of 12 months of continuous business operation to qualify for a standard small business loan. Businesses operating for 6-12 months may apply for the Early Stage Business Loan program with enhanced documentation requirements and a reduced maximum loan amount of PHP 500,000.

Businesses operating for less than 6 months are not eligible for any loan product at this time.

## Revenue Requirements

- **Standard Loan**: Minimum monthly revenue of PHP 100,000 (gross).
- **Early Stage Loan**: Minimum monthly revenue of PHP 50,000 (gross).
- Revenue must be verifiable through bank statements covering the most recent 6 months.
- Seasonal businesses may provide 12-month bank statements to demonstrate annualized revenue.

## Location Requirements

The business must be registered and primarily operating within the Philippines. Home-based businesses are eligible if they have a valid business permit from the local government unit (LGU).

Businesses operating in conflict-affected areas may face additional verification requirements.

## Collateral Requirements

- **Unsecured Loan** (up to PHP 500,000): No collateral required. Higher interest rate applies.
- **Secured Loan** (up to PHP 5,000,000): Acceptable collateral includes real property, vehicle, equipment, or inventory. Collateral must be appraised by an accredited appraiser.
- Loan-to-value ratio must not exceed 70% of appraised collateral value.

## Requested Loan Amount Limits

| Loan Product | Minimum | Maximum |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Business Loan | PHP 50,000 | PHP 5,000,000 |
| Early Stage Business Loan | PHP 20,000 | PHP 500,000 |
| Working Capital Line | PHP 100,000 | PHP 2,000,000 |

## Repayment Capacity

The applicant's debt-to-income ratio must not exceed 40%. This includes all existing obligations plus the proposed loan repayment.

Monthly net income must be at least 2.5 times the proposed monthly repayment amount.

## Disqualifying Conditions

- Outstanding defaults or delinquencies with any financial institution.
- Active legal proceedings related to fraud or financial crimes.
- Business engaged in prohibited activities (gambling, illegal operations).
- Applicant is below 21 years of age or above 65 years of age at loan maturity.
