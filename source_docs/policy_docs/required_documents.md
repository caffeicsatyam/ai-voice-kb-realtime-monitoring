# Required Documents for Business Loan Application

## Primary Documents (All Applicants)

1. **Valid Government-Issued ID** — Any two of the following: passport, driver's license, SSS/GSIS ID, PhilHealth ID, voter's ID, or postal ID.
2. **Business Registration** — DTI Certificate of Registration (sole proprietorship) or SEC Certificate of Registration (corporation/partnership).
3. **Business Permit** — Current Mayor's Permit or Business Permit from the local government unit.
4. **Tax Identification Number (TIN)** — BIR Certificate of Registration (Form 2303).
5. **Bank Statements** — Most recent 6 months of business bank account statements showing deposits and withdrawals.
6. **Financial Statements** — Most recent annual Income Statement and Balance Sheet. For loans above PHP 1,000,000, statements must be audited by a CPA.

## Additional Documents (Secured Loans)

7. **Collateral Documents** — Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) for real property, Certificate of Registration (CR) and Official Receipt (OR) for vehicles, or proof of ownership for equipment/inventory.
8. **Appraisal Report** — Property or asset appraisal by an accredited appraiser (not older than 6 months).

## Additional Documents (Early Stage Business Loan)

9. **Business Plan** — A one-page business plan describing the business model, target market, and growth projections.
10. **Proof of Business Activity** — Sales invoices, receipts, purchase orders, or contracts from the most recent 3 months.

## Why We Need Bank Statements

Bank statements are essential for verifying your actual business revenue and cash flow patterns. We use them to:
- Confirm monthly revenue matches your declared income.
- Assess cash flow stability and seasonal patterns.
- Verify there are no undisclosed obligations or unusual transactions.
- Calculate your repayment capacity accurately.

This is a standard requirement across all lending institutions and helps us offer you the most appropriate loan terms.

## Document Submission Guidelines

- All documents must be clear, legible copies. Original documents may be requested for verification.
- Digital submissions are accepted in PDF, JPG, or PNG format.
- Documents in languages other than English or Filipino must include a certified translation.
- Expired IDs or permits will not be accepted.
- Processing begins only after all required documents are received.
