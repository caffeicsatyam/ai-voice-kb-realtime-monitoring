# Business Loan Pricing and Fees

## Interest Rates

| Loan Product | Secured Rate | Unsecured Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Business Loan | 1.2% - 1.8% per month | 2.0% - 2.5% per month |
| Early Stage Business Loan | 1.5% - 2.0% per month | 2.5% - 3.0% per month |
| Working Capital Line | 1.5% per month | 2.2% per month |

Interest rates are computed on a diminishing balance basis. The exact rate depends on the applicant's credit profile, collateral value, business performance, and loan amount.

## Why Our Interest Rates Are Competitive

Our rates may appear higher than traditional bank rates, but we offer advantages that banks typically do not:
- Faster approval process (5-7 business days vs. 4-8 weeks at banks).
- Lower documentation requirements for loans under PHP 500,000.
- No requirement for 3 years of business operation.
- Flexible repayment schedules tailored to your cash flow.
- No hidden charges or surprise fees.

## Processing Fees

- **Application Fee**: None.
- **Processing Fee**: 2% of approved loan amount (one-time, deducted from disbursement).
- **Documentary Stamp Tax**: As required by BIR regulations.
- **Appraisal Fee** (secured loans): PHP 2,000 - PHP 5,000 depending on asset type and location.
- **Insurance** (secured loans): Based on collateral value, arranged through accredited partners.

## Late Payment Penalties

- **Grace Period**: 5 calendar days after due date.
- **Late Payment Fee**: 3% of the unpaid monthly amortization.
- **Consecutive Late Payments**: Three consecutive late payments may trigger loan restructuring or collection proceedings.

## Early Repayment

- No prepayment penalty for partial or full early repayment.
- Interest is computed only on the outstanding balance.
- Early repayment reduces total interest cost.

## Disbursement

- Loan proceeds are disbursed via bank transfer to the applicant's registered business bank account.
- Disbursement typically occurs within 1-2 business days after final approval.
- For secured loans, disbursement occurs after collateral registration is completed.
