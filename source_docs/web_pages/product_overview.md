# QuickFund Small Business Loan

## Fuel Your Business Growth

QuickFund offers fast, flexible financing solutions designed specifically for Filipino small business owners. Whether you need working capital, equipment financing, or funds to expand your operations, we have a loan product that fits your needs.

### Our Loan Products

**Standard Business Loan**
For established businesses with 12+ months of operation. Borrow from PHP 50,000 to PHP 5,000,000 with competitive rates and flexible terms from 6 to 36 months.

**Early Stage Business Loan**
New business? No problem. If you have been operating for 6-12 months, you can access up to PHP 500,000 to help your business grow.

**Working Capital Line**
A revolving credit facility from PHP 100,000 to PHP 2,000,000 for businesses that need ongoing access to funds for inventory, payroll, or seasonal expenses.

### How to Apply

1. Check your eligibility using our online tool or call us.
2. Prepare your documents (business registration, IDs, bank statements).
3. Submit your application online or at a partner branch.
4. Receive a decision within 5-7 business days.
5. Sign the loan agreement and receive your funds.
