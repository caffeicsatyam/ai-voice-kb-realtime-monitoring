# QuickFund Small Business Loan

## Fuel Your Business Growth

QuickFund offers fast, flexible financing designed for Filipino small business owners. Whether you need working capital, equipment financing, or expansion funds, we have loan products for your needs.

### Our Products

**Standard Business Loan**
For established businesses with 12+ months of operation. Borrow PHP 50,000 to PHP 5,000,000 with competitive rates and flexible terms of 6-36 months.

**Early Stage Business Loan**  
New business? If you have operated for 6-12 months, access up to PHP 500,000 for growth.

**Working Capital Line**
Revolving credit from PHP 100,000 to PHP 2,000,000 for inventory, payroll, or seasonal needs.
