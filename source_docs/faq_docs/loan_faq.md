# Frequently Asked Questions — QuickFund Business Loans

## Application Process

### How long does the approval process take?
The typical approval timeline is 5-7 business days from the date all required documents are received. Incomplete submissions may take longer. For urgent needs, ask about our expedited processing option (additional fee may apply).

### Can I apply online?
Yes, you can start your application through our website or mobile app. You will need to upload digital copies of your documents. Our loan specialist will contact you within 1 business day to confirm receipt and discuss next steps.

### What happens after I apply?
After submission, our team reviews your documents and conducts a credit assessment. You may be contacted for additional information or clarification. Once approved, you will receive a formal loan offer with the terms and conditions for your review and acceptance.

### Can I check the status of my application?
Yes, you can track your application status through our online portal or by calling our customer service hotline at 1-800-LOAN. Your loan specialist can also provide updates via email or SMS.

## Eligibility

### Can a business operating for 8 months qualify?
Yes, businesses operating for 6-12 months can apply for our Early Stage Business Loan program. The maximum loan amount is PHP 500,000, and you will need to provide additional documentation including a simple business plan and proof of recent business activity.

### Do I need collateral?
Collateral is not required for loans up to PHP 500,000 (unsecured). For larger amounts, acceptable collateral includes real property, vehicles, equipment, or inventory. The loan-to-value ratio must not exceed 70%.

### What if I have an existing loan with another bank?
Having existing loans does not automatically disqualify you. However, your total debt-to-income ratio must not exceed 40%. We will consider all existing obligations when assessing your repayment capacity.

### Are home-based businesses eligible?
Yes, home-based businesses are eligible as long as they have a valid business permit from the local government unit. The same eligibility criteria apply regarding business age and revenue.

## Loan Terms

### What repayment terms are available?
Repayment terms range from 6 to 36 months depending on the loan product and amount. We offer monthly, bi-monthly, or quarterly repayment schedules to match your business cash flow cycle.

### Is there a penalty for early repayment?
No, there is no prepayment penalty. You can make partial or full early repayments at any time, and interest is computed only on the outstanding balance.

### What happens if I miss a payment?
A 5-day grace period is provided after the due date. After the grace period, a late payment fee of 3% of the unpaid amortization applies. Three consecutive late payments may trigger loan restructuring or collection proceedings. If you anticipate difficulty making a payment, contact us immediately to discuss options.

## Security and Privacy

### Is my information secure?
Yes, we follow strict data privacy protocols in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173). Your personal and financial information is encrypted and accessible only to authorized personnel involved in processing your application.

### Will applying affect my credit score?
We conduct a soft credit inquiry during the initial assessment, which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry is only performed after you accept a formal loan offer.
