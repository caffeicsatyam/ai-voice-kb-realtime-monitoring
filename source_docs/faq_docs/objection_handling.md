# Objection Handling Guide for Loan Agents

## Interest Rate Objections

### "Your interest rates are too high."

**Handling approach**: Acknowledge the concern, then explain the value proposition.

Suggested response: "I understand your concern about the interest rate. Our rates reflect the speed and flexibility we offer compared to traditional banks. While bank rates may appear lower, they typically require 3+ years of business history, extensive documentation, and 4-8 weeks for approval. We can approve qualified applicants in just 5-7 business days with simpler requirements. We also have no prepayment penalty, so you can reduce your total interest cost by paying early."

### "Can you lower the rate?"

**Handling approach**: Explain what affects the rate and offer alternatives.

Suggested response: "The rate is based on several factors including your business profile, loan amount, and whether you provide collateral. Secured loans have lower rates — typically 1.2% to 1.8% per month compared to 2.0% to 2.5% for unsecured loans. If you can provide collateral, I can help you explore that option for a better rate."

## Document Objections

### "Why do you need my bank statements?"

**Handling approach**: Explain the specific purpose transparently.

Suggested response: "Bank statements help us verify your actual business cash flow and determine the right loan amount and terms for your situation. We look at your deposits to confirm revenue, check for stability, and ensure the repayment amount fits your cash flow. This is a standard requirement across lending institutions and ultimately helps us offer you terms that work for your business."

### "I don't have all the documents right now."

**Handling approach**: Be helpful, not dismissive.

Suggested response: "That is completely fine. Let me note which documents you do have and which ones you still need to prepare. I can also explain exactly what we need for each document so you know what to bring. Would you like me to schedule a follow-up call for when you have everything ready?"

## Eligibility Objections

### "I was rejected by a bank. Will you reject me too?"

**Handling approach**: Differentiate the product without guaranteeing approval.

Suggested response: "Our eligibility criteria are different from traditional banks. We work with businesses as young as 6 months old, and we focus on your current business performance rather than just your credit history. I cannot guarantee approval before we review your complete application, but I can walk you through our requirements right now so you know where you stand."

### "My business is seasonal — my revenue varies a lot."

**Handling approach**: Show flexibility.

Suggested response: "We understand that many businesses have seasonal patterns. For seasonal businesses, we can look at 12 months of bank statements to assess your annualized revenue. We also offer flexible repayment schedules — monthly, bi-monthly, or quarterly — that can be aligned with your peak and off-peak periods."

## Trust and Process Objections

### "How do I know this is legitimate?"

**Handling approach**: Provide verifiable credentials.

Suggested response: "QuickFund Lending Corporation is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and licensed by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP). You can verify our registration on the SEC and BSP websites. We also comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 to protect your personal information."

### "I want to talk to a human / a manager."

**Handling approach**: Respect the request immediately.

Suggested response: "Of course. Let me connect you with one of our senior loan specialists who can assist you further. May I have your preferred contact number and a convenient time for the callback?"

Action: Log escalation request, collect callback details, and transfer to human agent queue.
